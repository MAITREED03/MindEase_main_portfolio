About The Project




MindEase is an AI-powered mental health support app. It helps users analyze their emotions, receive empathetic responses, and keep a private journal of their thoughts.

Features include:
1. Real-time Emotion Detection (happy, sad, anxious, hopeful, etc.)
2. Contextual Supportive Responses
3. Privacy-First Offline Journaling
4. Journal History & Downloads

MindEase is designed to be simple, private, and supportive for daily mental well-being.
